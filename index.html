A Mission for All of NC.

360ENC specializes in building healthy communities through hands-on education and systemic support. We work across three fundamental pillars to ensure every North Carolinian has equal access to services and the skills required to save lives. From the coast to the mountains, our mission remains the same: ensuring readiness for all.

Community Health

Providing CPR, First Aid, and FAFOO training in partnership with Trillium Health Resources across all NC counties.

Civic Engagement

Empowering residents statewide to organize, participate in local governance, and strengthen community resilience.

Advocacy & Policy

Dismantling systemic barriers to create long-term equity and ensure resources reach those who need them most.
