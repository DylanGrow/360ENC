Ready is being Prepared.

360ENC specializes in building healthy communities through hands-on education and systemic support. We work across three fundamental pillars to ensure Eastern North Carolina has equal access to services and the skills to save lives.

Community Health

Providing life-saving skills like CPR, First Aid, and FAFOO training in partnership with Trillium Health Resources.

Civic Engagement

Empowering residents to organize, participate in local governance, and strengthen community resilience.

Advocacy & Policy

Addressing systemic barriers to create long-term equity and ensure resources reach those who need them most.
