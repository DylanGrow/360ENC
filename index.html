Ready is being Prepared.

Based in New Bern, 360ENC specializes in hands-on education and systemic support. We bridge the gap between emergency needs and community resources through three core pillars.

Community Health

Life-saving certification courses including CPR, First Aid, and FAFOO in collaboration with Trillium Health Resources.

Civic Engagement

Resources to help residents organize, advocate, and strengthen local community resilience from the ground up.

Advocacy & Policy

Removing systemic barriers to ensure health equity and resource accessibility across Eastern North Carolina.
